Number Systems

Number Systems

  • Number Systems are the different types of number bases that are being utilized in the field of computing. (Kneusel, 2016). Knowing the different number systems are essential for computer programmers for its applications extend beyond the tech world (Jena, 2021).

Types of Number Systems

Binary (1010010)

  • Binary numbers are the foundation of computer systems, where they represent data through two states: on (1) and off (0). (O’Regan, 2018). Binary allows for computers to manipulate and store data through the system of switches represented by ones and zeroes (Zirkind, 2016).

Octal (e.g 251)

  • BThe octal system uses eight symbols: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7 (Rawat et al., 2012). It makes it easier to convert between binary (base-2) and decimal (base-10) systems since each octal digit corresponds to three binary digits (Rawat et al. 2012).

Decimal (e.g 169)

  • The Decimal System employs ten different digits to represent a number (0-9). This is the most commonly used number system by humans (Jena, 2021).

Hexadecimal (e.g A9)

  • The hexadecimal system is a base-16 numbering system composed of 16 symbols (0-9 and A-F) (Pascaud, 1986). This system is widely used in computing and digital electronics due to its efficiency in representing binary data (Weng, 2000)

Significance and uses of each number system

Significance of Binary

  • Binary numbers are the foundation of computer systems, where they represent data through two states: on (1) and off (0). (O’Regan, 2018). Binary allows for computers to manipulate and store data through the system of switches represented by ones and zeroes (Zirkind, 2016).

Significance of Octals

  • The octal number system is heavily utilized in Pattern Coding. Medical diagnostics use octal numbers to represent complicated data patterns, such as protein electrophoresis. Each octal digit represents a particular physiological state, making computer-assisted interpretative reporting possible (Dito, 1977).
  • In electronic design, the octal number system are utilized through octal clocks which are employed in data capture systems, where they help synchronize data sampling with reference clock edges, improving the accuracy of clock/data recovery systems (Pickering et al., 2013). Octal numbers are also utilized in traffic control systems to create unique intersection identities based on octet values, which simplifies traffic management (Sook, 2010).

Significance of Decimals

  • Decimal numbers require no prior introduction, they have heavily used in our daily lives. We use them so often that it almost makes them part of the nature of our lives (Kneusel, 2017).
  • The significance of the decimal systems extends across various fields, from mathematics to computing and software engineering. The decimal system is what makes consists of our main counting numbers (Jena, 2021).

Significance of Hexadecimal

  • Hexadecimals are involved in many applications of computing and data processing . The hexadecimal number system helps make long binary numbers be compressed into smaller bits and making it much more efficient for data representation. Hexadecimals make debugging for programmers easier as they can make long binary data more readable. Hexadecimals are also commonly used in color coding in web design and photo editing due to their convenience in data representation (Natarajan, 2020).
