Welcome to InPoCoS!

   Information Portfolios on Computer Systems (InPoCoS) is an educational website designed to serve as a comprehensive resource for users seeking to expand their knowledge in various topics related to computer systems. Whether you're a student, professional, or curious learner, InPoCoS provides well-organized portfolio guides tailored to facilitate effective and structured learning. The content on InPoCoS is curated from reliable and authoritative sources, ensuring that users have access to accurate, up-to-date information. Each guide is carefully structured to provide clarity and depth, breaking down complex concepts into digestible sections. The aim is to empower users with the skills and knowledge necessary to understand, analyze, and apply what they learn in real-world scenarios.


InPoCoS covers a wide range of topics which are clustered into portfolios. These topics include:

  • Computer Science as a Discipline, Computing Disciplines and Majors
  • Data, Information, and Information Systems
  • Number Systems
  • Computer Hardware and Software: Their Implementation in the different institutions in the community
  • Comparative Study on Different types of Motherboards
  • Comparative Study on Different types of Computers
